Clinical summary

Clinical Summary

Jon was taken to casualty and admitted to a medical ward two weeks ago after taking a serious overdose of paracetamol and drinking half a bottle of whisky.

He was found unconscious face down among the bushes in a local park.

Jon had been depressed for some months prior to this having lost his job as a building surveyor.

He was having difficulty sleeping and often waking early, sometimes at three of four in the morning.

While on the ward his suicidal urges have reduced and he has been started on an atypical antidepressant to good effect.

He now regrets attempting to take his own life, is thankful for the support of his family and looks forward to commencing psychological therapies for depression (IAPT) and seeking new employment.
